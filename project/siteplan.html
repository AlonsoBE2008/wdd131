Homemade recipes

A simple website for sharing easy-to-prepare recipes at home.

Site Name

Homemade recipes – I chose this name because it clearly reflects the purpose of the site: to show simple and accessible recipes for anyone.

Optional domain: recetas-caseras.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a small digital recipe book. It will provide step-by-step instructions, ingredient lists, and illustrative photos for each recipe.

Scenarios

  • What can I cook quickly for dinner with few ingredients?
  • How do I prepare basic pancakes for breakfast?

Color Schema

Colores seleccionados:

  • Verde (#2E8B57) – Used in headings and accents.
  • Beige (#F5F5DC) – Used as a background color.

Typography

Fuentes seleccionadas:

  • Roboto – For the body of the text.
  • Lobster – For titles and headings.
