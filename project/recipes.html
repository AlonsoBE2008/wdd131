Classic Pancakes

Prep time: 10 minutes · Cook time: 15 minutes · Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat egg with milk and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed; small lumps are fine.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.

Tips

Do not overmix the batter. Serve with fresh fruit or maple syrup.

Fresh Garden Salad

Prep time: 10 minutes · Serves: 2-3

Ingredients

  • 1 head of lettuce, washed and torn
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine lettuce, tomato, and cucumber in a bowl.
  2. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make dressing.
  3. Toss salad with dressing and serve immediately.

Tips

Add toasted nuts or crumbled cheese for extra flavor and texture.
